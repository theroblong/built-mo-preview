Summary: Forecasting Accuracy and Operating Model

The demand intelligence report provides the broadest technical and business evidence: why growth-mode forecasting is hard, how Mo models the drivers, how it validates across time, and how the plan translates into revenue, inventory, and trade spend use cases.

The Planning Problem

BUILT is not a steady-state brand. Distribution expansion can make total units rise even when per-store velocity is flat. A simple trend forecast can mistake new doors for durable demand and overstate the plan when distribution growth slows.

Growth-mode distortion: Pure trend extrapolation asks whether the trend continues. Your planning question is different: is the trend coming from real demand, more stores, promotion, price, or portfolio mix?
