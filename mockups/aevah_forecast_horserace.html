Why not just ask ChatGPT — or use Salesforce, or another forecast platform?
You can ask ChatGPT anything about the protein bar category and get a thoughtful answer. But it cannot tell you what your 4-pack did at Kroger last quarter, or why your 12-pack is underperforming at Walmart while it is growing at Target, or whether your next promotional event will pull volume from your own single-serve. General AI tools answer questions about the world. Mo answers questions about your business — because it is trained on BUILT's actual weekly sales data, across every SKU, every retailer, every channel. The result is a forecast that responds to your dynamics, not industry averages.
