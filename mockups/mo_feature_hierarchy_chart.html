Defend Price

Use when BUILT is priced above a competitor but velocity, distribution, and pack role remain healthy.

Test Value Correction

Use when the 12-pack or value pack is above category norm and store penetration is weak.

Monitor

Use when launch or price evidence is mixed, early, or confounded by TDP and promo changes.

Expand Winner

Use when base demand and productivity rise without meaningful donor decline in the same market.

Reduce Overlap

Use when the focal SKU rises while same-flavor donor SKUs fall and net productivity weakens.

Read left to right within each objective: Determine finds the event, Diagnose proves or rejects the mechanism, Decide converts evidence into a commercial action.
